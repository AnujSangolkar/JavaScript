Welcome to Sangolkar fruit Company - Masters of Banana Ripening Since 2009

At SFC, we don't just deal in bananas; we cultivate a legacy that spans three generations of expertise in the art and science of banana ripening. Our journey began 50 years ago with a passion for delivering the finest, ripest bananas to households and businesses around the globe.

Why Choose SFC?

  • Generational Expertise: Our knowledge has been passed down through three generations, ensuring a wealth of experience in every banana we ripen. Our commitment to excellence is deeply rooted in our family's history.
  • Facilities: We pride ourselves on our modern and technologically advanced ripening facilities. Using cutting-edge methods, we create the perfect conditions to achieve the optimal ripeness, flavor, and texture that discerning customers expect.
  • Sustainable Practices: Respect for the environment is at the core of our operations. We employ eco-friendly practices throughout the ripening process, ensuring that every banana reaches you with the least environmental impact possible.
  • Quality Assurance: Our rigorous quality control measures guarantee that only the finest bananas bear the SFC stamp of approval. From sourcing to ripening, we maintain the highest standards to meet and exceed your expectations.

Our Commitment to You

Whether you're a retailer looking for top-tier produce or a consumer seeking the perfect banana for your family, SFC is your trusted partner. We understand that the journey from the banana plantation to your table is a critical one, and we take great care at every step to ensure freshness, flavor, and satisfaction.
